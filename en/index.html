    LED Control Kit

    The LED-BUG SR is a lighting controller that uses an ESP32 as its main controller. It supports various voltage RGB light strips and is equipped with built-in WLED firmware, which enables music rhythm and DMX512 control. The board integrates level conversion, a digital microphone, and a MAX485 chip. It also provides reserved GPIO and power output, suitable for connecting sensors and actuators. It supports development with Arduino IDE and Platform IO.

    Easily achieve music rhythm and colorful lighting effects, supporting a wide range of light strips and DMX512 devices, compatible with advanced ESP32 development. Unleash your creativity and unlock endless possibilities in lighting art!

    Product Features

    Plug and Play

    No software installation or configuration on the target computer needed, fast to use.

    Multi-Voltage Compatibility

    Supports 5V, 12V, 24V programmable light strips, adaptable to various power supplies.

    Music Rhythm Support

    On-board digital microphone, supports sound collection, achieving music rhythm effects.

    Flexible Light Strip Control

    Supports various programmable light strips such as WS281X, SK6812, TM1814, with dual light strip signal outputs.

    Strong Extensibility

    On-board MAX485 chip, supports DMX512 protocol, suitable for various lighting projects.

    Custom Programming

    Main control uses ESP32, supports development environments like Arduino, TouchDesigner, Platform IO, etc.
